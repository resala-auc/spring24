Events

Save a life!

A four-day blood drive where members of the AUC community save hundreds of lives by donating blood, a collaboration with Resala and the Ministry of Health and Population.

A mega event during Ramadan as a collaboration between Resala and other AUC entities to pack food for those who are in need in El-Deweika. Together, we impacted 600 families in Ramadan 2024.

Orphan's Day

A day filled with fun activities and games that brought smiles to their faces as well as food and snacks. We provided the 36 children of El-Deweika with clothes and toys, adding to their happiness.

Visits to Resala's branch in Al-Nozha for packing food (It3am) and sorting and filtering medical supplies and clothes.
